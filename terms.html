Terms of use

Response Rehearsal is provided as an educational and planning aid for defensive incident response tabletop exercises. By using the tool, you are responsible for reviewing, adapting, and validating any generated material before relying on it.

No professional advice

Generated content is not legal, compliance, regulatory, insurance, security, or incident-response advice. Consult appropriate professionals and your organization's approved plans, policies, contracts, and legal obligations before making decisions.

Facilitator responsibility

Facilitators should confirm that scenarios are appropriate for the audience, remove sensitive details, avoid creating panic or confusion, and document exercise outcomes according to their own organizational policies.

No guarantees

The tool does not guarantee exercise quality, incident readiness, compliance outcomes, business continuity, security improvement, or incident response success. Use it as a starting point, not as a final authority.

Appropriate use

Use the generator for defensive training and planning. Do not use it to create operational attack guidance, collect sensitive data, or simulate real incidents in a way that misleads participants or third parties.
